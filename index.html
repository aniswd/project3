about our
company

Three generations of the NOMAD family have carefully looked after the freight and trucking needs of the New US area.

air
freight

Air freight is the transportation of goods by air. It offers speed, reliability, and efficiency, making it ideal for time-sensitive or perishable items.

sea
freight

Sea freight, also known as ocean freight, refers to the transportation of goods by sea. It is one of the most cost-effective and widely used modes.

ground
cargo

Ground cargo refers to the transportation of goods over land using various modes of transportation, such as trucks, trains, or vans.

packaging
options

Packaging options refer to the various methods and materials used to protect and contain goods for storage, transportation, and sale.

project
logistics

Project logistics refers to the complex coordination and management of all logistical activities involved in executing large-scale projects.

supply chain
management

Supply chain management refers to the coordination and oversight of all activities involved in the flow of goods and services to the final delivery to customers.

warehousing
service

Warehousing services play a critical role in the logistics and supply chain industry by providing secure and efficient storage solutions for goods and products.
